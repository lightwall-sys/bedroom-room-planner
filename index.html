Rotation, alignment and modular placement
The main rotate controls always move in 90° steps. Use the Shift controls for 5° adjustments, or type an exact angle in Add furniture. Wardrobes automatically align their backs flush to nearby walls. EKET cabinets can join into modular wall arrangements. Manual rotation or the visible alignment control overrides automatic behaviour.
