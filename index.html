Rotation and wardrobe snapping
The main rotate controls always move in 90° steps. Use the Shift controls for 5° adjustments, or type an exact angle in Add furniture. Wardrobes automatically align their backs flush to nearby walls. Any manual rotation switches that wardrobe to manual mode; press W or the visible wall-snap button to restore automatic alignment.
