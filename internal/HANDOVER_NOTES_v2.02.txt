IMPORTANT RENDER-LIFECYCLE RULE
-------------------------------
There must remain only one authority that calls requestAnimationFrame(renderFrame): scheduleRenderFrame().

Do not:
- set renderQueued to false and then allow controls.update() to schedule independently
- add a second continuous animation loop for OrbitControls
- call requestAnimationFrame(renderFrame) directly from the change handler

The change handler may request a render. renderInProgress prevents that request from creating a competing callback while the current callback is executing.

CUTAWAY MAINTENANCE RULE
------------------------
Do not reintroduce a camera-height rule that hides every wall. Elevated Design views must still retain a directional cutaway.

Explicit behaviour:
- cutaway: hide one directional wall section
- up: hide none
- down: hide all
- plan view: hide all

The right-hand stepped boundary is represented by sections 2, 3 and 4 and must continue to switch as one group (w234).

WALL MATERIAL AND SHADOW RULE
-----------------------------
The walls are intentionally plain and do not use a generated or image texture. The v2.02 anti-banding treatment depends on:
- wall material dithering
- wall material shadowSide control
- positive spotlight shadow bias
- spotlight normalBias

If lighting is redesigned, check large wall surfaces at shallow camera angles and at sunset/evening times before reducing those offsets.

ROTATION USER EXPERIENCE
------------------------
Rotation must remain visible without requiring keyboard knowledge or opening Add furniture after every selection.

Contextual banner behaviour:
- pending floor item: two rotation buttons
- pending wall item: Switch orientation
- selected floor item in Move objects mode: two rotation buttons and current angle
- selected wall item in Move objects mode: Switch orientation
- no selection: guidance only

Q, E and R are secondary shortcuts, not the primary discoverability mechanism.

RESPONSIVE BEHAVIOUR
--------------------
- At wider widths, the contextual banner remains a single compact row when space permits.
- Below 680 px, banner copy and action buttons wrap into two rows.
- Existing drawer, taskbar and mobile layout breakpoints are unchanged.

MANUAL POST-DEPLOYMENT QA
-------------------------
1. Hard-refresh the published Pages URL and confirm v2.02 appears in the header and Help panel.
2. Orbit continuously for at least 20 seconds and confirm frame pacing remains stable.
3. Orbit above the previous height threshold and confirm only one directional wall section is hidden.
4. Rotate through diagonal views and confirm cutaway walls do not flicker rapidly.
5. Test Walls up, Cutaway walls, Walls down and Plan view.
6. Add each floor item and use the visible banner buttons before placement.
7. Select an existing floor item in Move objects mode and rotate it from the banner.
8. Add/select a wall item and use Switch orientation.
9. Inspect all walls at 06:30, 14:00 and 19:30 for horizontal shadow striping.
10. Reopen the page and confirm the v2.02 service worker has replaced older caches.
