RENDER-LIFECYCLE RULE
---------------------
Keep scheduleRenderFrame() as the only authority that calls requestAnimationFrame(renderFrame).

Do not:
- add a second continuous render loop for OrbitControls
- call requestAnimationFrame(renderFrame) directly from the controls change handler
- clear renderQueued early enough for controls.update() to create a competing callback

The camera performance repair from v2.02 is confirmed as accepted and must not be regressed.

WALL RENDERING RULE
-------------------
Visible wall meshes intentionally do not cast shadows. This is the root v2.03 fix for the repeating horizontal/stepped shadow-map pattern.

Maintain:
- wall castShadow = false
- wall receiveShadow = true
- no generated or image wall texture
- wall roughness = 1
- material dithering
- neutral C.wall_white for every wall except the selected accent wall

Do not re-enable wall shadow casting without testing every wall at shallow angles with the overhead light enabled. Positive bias alone did not remove the pattern in v2.02.

ACCENT-WALL RULE
----------------
Exactly one selected wall may use the chosen Dulux accent approximation. setAccentWall() must continue to iterate through every wall material and reset all non-selected walls to C.wall_white.

ROTATION AND MOVEMENT RULE
--------------------------
Primary rotation is deliberately orthogonal:
- Q / E: 90 degrees
- main visible buttons: 90 degrees
- Shift+Q / Shift+E: 5 degrees
- visible fine buttons: 5 degrees
- R: nearest 90 degrees

Primary movement:
- arrows: active grid size
- Shift+arrows: 1 cm

Do not tie rotation steps back to the position grid-snap toggle. Position and angle refinement are separate user intentions in v2.03.

DELETION RULE
-------------
Every deletion path must finish with a shadow refresh because renderer.shadowMap.autoUpdate is false.

removeSelectedItem() currently:
- hides the selection helper
- removes registered item meshes
- removes and disposes the group
- marks shadowDirty
- sets renderer.shadowMap.needsUpdate
- calls requestRender(true, 3)
- queues history

Right-click is reserved for OrbitControls panning. Do not bind it to deletion.

CONTROL-DISCOVERABILITY RULE
----------------------------
The planner has three layers of control visibility:
1. labelled Controls button in the taskbar
2. persistent Essential controls card on wider screens
3. complete Controls panel plus contextual placement/selection actions

When adding a shortcut, update all relevant layers and the public README. Do not introduce hidden keyboard-only functionality for core editing actions.

DIMPLEX QUANTUM PROXY
---------------------
The heater is an approximate late-2010s Dimplex Quantum visual proxy using a dimension-matched casing allowance.

Preserve the recognisable design cues:
- plain white main front
- subtle vertical pressed channels
- low fan outlet grille with repeated dark slots
- white end cheeks and small side vents
- two rounded feet
- closed top-right control lid

Do not claim that a precise wattage/model variant has been identified. The source record explains the casing match and uncertainty.

- Drawers and existing mobile taskbar behaviour are retained.
